Information Collection and Use

Our app is designed to be simple and requires no user login or personal account creation. However, to improve our Service, we use third-party services that may collect information.

  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand which features are most popular, how users interact with the app, and where we can make improvements. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
  • Crash Reporting: We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect information about crashes and errors. This helps us find and fix bugs to improve the app's stability. The reports include information about your device type and OS version but do not contain personal data.
  • User-Generated Data (Favorites): To provide the "Favorites" feature, the app uses a local Room Database on your device to store the IDs of the poses you have favorited. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Service Providers

Remember that all your personal preference data (Favorites) is stored only on your device.
